# About Me

I am a Computer Engineering candidate at the University of Waterloo. I have an deep appreciation for novel ideas/technologies and try to ensure that my learning never stagnates. I have a wide variety of interests ranging from software development to music and current affairs.

I have completed internships at organizations of various sizes, the most recent placement being a Toronto based startup. The majority of my responsibilities have been oriented towards web development using technologies across the stack.

One skill that I pride myself on is the ability to empathize with a client/customer's needs and maintain a solid focus on the projects that I work on. Another important skill that I have cultivated in my past work experiences is the ability to communicate within a development team and to other departments/external clients. Opportunities that demonstrate these skills have manifested in the form of presentations to a company providing product updates on behalf of the development team. Another way has been through attending client meetings to gather business requirements and translating that information into technical specifications.

As a TCK (Third Culture Kid), I have lived in a number of countries since birth, which has offered me a unique perspective of the world and what it means to be a global citizen.
